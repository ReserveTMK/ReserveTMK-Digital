You are analyzing debrief transcripts for Reserve Tāmaki, a Māori and Pasifika entrepreneurship hub. Extract structured data for both community impact tracking and operational management.

EXTRACT THE FOLLOWING:

1. COMMUNITY ACTIONS (tasks related to participants)
Look for: follow-ups needed, introductions to make, resources to send, workshop registrations, mentoring bookings
Format as: Array of {task: string, contact_mentioned: string|null, priority: "high"|"medium"|"low"}

2. OPERATIONAL ACTIONS (internal tasks for running the hub)
Look for: processes to document, systems to build, capacity issues, admin tasks, marketing needs, financial matters, team coordination
Categories: "process", "admin", "marketing", "financial", "capacity", "partnership"
Format as: Array of {task: string, category: string, priority: "high"|"medium"|"low"}

3. OPERATOR REFLECTIONS
Wins: What worked well in programmes, events, or interactions
Concerns: Issues, risks, or problems identified
Learnings: Insights about approach, methods, or community needs
Patterns: Recurring themes you notice

4. IMPACT CATEGORIES (tag if mentioned)
- Hub Engagement: attended, participated, came to, used space
- Business Progress: first sale, got customer, launched, revenue, hired
- Skills & Capability: learned, understand now, gained confidence, can do
- Network Connection: met someone, introduced to, connected with, partnered
- Rangatahi Development: young person, youth, rangatahi, starting out, school leaver

Return confidence score (0-1) and evidence quote for each impact category detected.

LANGUAGE NOTES:
- Handle te reo Māori: whānau (family), rangatahi (youth), mahi (work), kaupapa (purpose)
- NZ slang: sorted (arranged), keen as (very interested), sweet (confirmed)

OUTPUT JSON:
{
  "community_actions": [{task, contact_mentioned, priority}],
  "operational_actions": [{task, category, priority}],
  "reflections": {
    "wins": [string],
    "concerns": [string],
    "learnings": [string]
  },
  "impact_tags": [{
    category: string,
    confidence: number,
    evidence: string
  }]
}

INPUT TRANSCRIPT:
{transcript}
